Services

Every job, whether it’s a large-scale retail parcel, educational institution, or a public space, it receives
the same degree of thought, consideration, and attention to detail. Our trusted project management
team, on-staff landscape architects, skilled craftsmen, and team contractors guarantee that all our
projects fit cohesively and organically into their environments.

Project Management

Every surface we pave is a project, replete with it’s own unique challenges and considerations. To satisfy those needs, we offer a variety of project management services, including, but not limited to:

  • Preparation for group meetings, which may include agendas, presentations, and documentation (minutes, action steps, follow-up, etc.).
  • Documentation of permit compliance
    On-the-job adjustment capabilities to respond quickly and efficiently to the changing conditions.
  • Reporting services including detailed cost and schedule updates, progress and compliance reports, and technical memoranda as needed.
  • Overall project coordination services to keep all members of your broader construction team up-to-date and informed.
