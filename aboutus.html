TRUSTED PARTNER

We believe we are only as strong as our network; we
maintain strong relationships with leaders of every
corner the construction market, starting with our
clients and developers down to our trusted team of
subcontractors and vendors.

Started in 2011, Surfaces Group has grown to find itself servicing
the commercial hardscape and public spaces construction
markets throughout the Southeast. With a focus on client
satisfaction and innovative solutions, Surfaces Group has
provided hardscape solutions for both commercial and public
clients.

Surfaces Group routinely takes part in industry events to
maintain a knowledge of all current and innovative aspects of
the hardscape industry. Surfaces Group received the 2016
Hardscapes North America award in the Concrete Paver –
Permeable Pavers category. We plan to build on our
award-winning abilities by continuing to deliver successful
projects for our clients.
